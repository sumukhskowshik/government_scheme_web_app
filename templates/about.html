About Govt Schemes Portal

Building a transparent and citizen-friendly digital governance platform

The Govt Schemes Web Portal is a centralized digital platform developed to simplify access to government welfare schemes for citizens. The system enables users to explore schemes, verify eligibility, apply online and track application progress efficiently.

The platform also supports administrators with powerful tools to manage schemes, review applications, monitor user activities, and respond to citizen queries transparently. Our objective is to improve accessibility, transparency and efficiency in government service delivery.

Our Vision

To create a secure, transparent and fully digital ecosystem where citizens can easily access government welfare services from anywhere at any time.
